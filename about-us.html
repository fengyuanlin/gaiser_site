About Us

Established in 1963. Gaiser Comfort Solutions is proud to serve our local area which includes Tipton County, IN, Tipton, IN, Kempton, IN, Sharpsville, IN, Elwood, IN, Hobbs, IN, Windfall, IN, Atlanta, IN, Cicero, IN, and Arcadia, IN.

Gaiser Comfort Solutions, in Tipton, IN, is the leading heating and cooling company serving Kempton, Hobbs, Sharpsville, Windfall, Elwood, Atlanta and surrounding areas since 1963. We offer residential heating and cooling service, installation and repair, geothermal heating and cooling, air-source heat pumps, electric furnaces, humidifiers, air cleaning, heated floors, boilers and much more. For all your heating and cooling needs, contact Gaiser Comfort Solutions in Tipton.

Our Family Beginnings

For more than two generations, Gaiser Comfort Solutions has evolved to provide services that people love. Our storied history began in Tipton, IN, when two men, Joe Gaiser and Ivan Smeltzer discovered their passion for HVAC and created a company that stood the test of time. However, in the more than 55 years since, we’ve set our sights on also making a better life for people. We're powered by the newest science, technology, and the desire to be bold in order to create a tailored living experience, today. As a family-owned business, we have the unique ability to have a big impact on the community. This also bestows us with the responsibility to make sure we do right by the many communities where we work and live — for the next hundred years and beyond.

Today, the son of Founder Joe Gaiser, Mark Gaiser, is still keeping the art of clean and efficient HVAC services alive at Gaiser's Comfort Solutions. With the skilled technicians and friendly faces that fill our building every day, we absolutely invite you to put your trust in us for your household needs.
